> [!note] Ability 1
> - [x] When you give medical care to [Heal](z_Obsi-Forge-Apedia/Moves/Recover/Heal.md) yourself or another character, add +1.
> 	- If you are treating someone other than yourself, on a hit take +1 spirit or +1 momentum
___

> - [ ] When you [[Gather Information]] by studying medical evidence or biological remains, add +1

> 	- On a strong hit with a match, you also reveal an unexpected medical anomaly; mark 1 tick on your discoveries legacy track.
___
