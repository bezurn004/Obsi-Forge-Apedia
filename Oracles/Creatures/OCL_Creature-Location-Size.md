## Oracle: Creature Location
> **Usage**
> Choose the closest match for your location. Or roll to identify the primary habitat of a creature.

| 1d100 | Result |
|:---:|:--- |
| 1-5 | Space |
| 6-15 | Interior |
| 16-55 | Land |
| 56-80 | Liquid |
| 81-100 | Air |
